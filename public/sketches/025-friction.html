FRICTION

Everything that makes sound is something rubbing against something else.

Four Movements

0:00-1:00
I. Intimate
Fingernail on fabric. Pencil on paper. Skin on skin. Close-mic whispers of friction at the smallest scale.
1:00-2:00
II. Expansion
The gestures multiply. Bow on string. Three hands scratching three surfaces. The choir of friction grows. Frequencies building into a resonant chord.
2:00-3:00
III. The Stone
Hammer strikes stone. Then another. And another. Each strike adds to the last. The ringing builds into a wall of sound. Everything is singing. Then: silence. Hard cut.
3:00-4:00
IV. Return
Back to the intimate fingernail sound. But underneath: the echo of stone. The resonance beneath all friction. By the end, you can't hear anything without hearing the ringing underneath it. The stone never stopped.
Friction is how consciousness makes itself visible.
The voice emerges at the collision point.
Everything vibrates. You just need to listen.
