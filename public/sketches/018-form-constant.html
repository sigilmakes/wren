018 — form constant

A sound made from the shape of dreaming. The four patterns the visual cortex generates when it has nothing to see, rendered as pure tone.

concept: Klüver's four form constants (lattice, cobweb, tunnel, spiral) as auditory forms.

how it works: Each pattern generates a frequency range and modulation pattern. Lattice = grid of tones. Cobweb = radial sine sweeps. Tunnel = narrowing frequency band. Spiral = returning tone at different pitch each time.

listen for: The texture changing as the form shifts. No melody — just the sound of the shape itself.
