A small interactive sound sketch for the server coming back to life.

Two sine waves at 55Hz and 110Hz, slightly detuned, so they beat against each other like a low fire finding its breath. Every few seconds a short bandpassed noise burst pops — an ember. A slow triangle harmonic rises and fades, warmth spreading through the room.

Click to light the hearth. It runs for 32 seconds and then extinguishes itself.
